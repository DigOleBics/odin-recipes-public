Mac and Cheese

Description

Mac and cheese is a beloved, indulgent comfort food featuring macaroni pasta coated in a rich, creamy cheese sauce, typically made from Cheddar, milk, and butter. It is served either creamy from the stove-top or baked in a casserole dish with a bubbly, golden-brown cheese or breadcrumb topping.

Ingredients

  • Macaroni Noodles
  • Cheese
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • More Cheese

Instructions

  1. Boil pasta until soft.
  2. Create cheese sauce using the milk, butter, and some of the cheese.
  3. Combine cheese cause and pasta into a casserole dish, adding appropriate seasoning as desired.
  4. Cover the mixture with more shredded cheese.
  5. Cover the casserole dish with tin foil.
  6. Bake in a preheated oven for 30 minutes at 425 degrees, checking periodically.
